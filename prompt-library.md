# Florist — Premium Image Prompt Library

> **Style Master Guide** — Apply to every prompt below.
>
> **Camera:** Canon EOS R5 / Sony A7RV, 85mm f/1.4, 50mm f/1.8, 24-70mm f/2.8
> **Lighting:** Soft natural window light, golden hour, diffused studio strobes with softboxes, subtle fill light
> **Color Grade:** Warm amber-gold shadows, desaturated emerald greens, rose-blush midtones, cream highlights
> **Aesthetic:** Editorial luxury, minimal clutter, shallow depth of field, premium Indian floral studio
> **Vibe:** Taj Hotels campaign / Good Earth / Forest Essentials — understated opulence
> **Background:** Clean textured walls, raw silk drapes, brushed brass surfaces, handcrafted ceramics, marble, linen
> **Avoid:** AI artifacts, distorted flowers, oversaturated colours, harsh shadows, cluttered frames, text, watermarks, logos, cartoon style, illustration, 3D render, digital art, surreal, fantasy, dark backgrounds
